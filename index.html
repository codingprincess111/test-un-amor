Our Story

Un Amor has brought culture to the brunch scene in the heart of Chicago. Our team of culinary enthusiasts showcase a menu of traditional recipes and innovative twists, designed to bring people together and experience true authenticity in every dish. Our restaurant offers the freshest quality meat sourced from Slagel Farm, and fresh in-house bakery goods.
